About Me


My name is Barbora, and I am passionate about improving the lives of others through good design. I’m constantly learning new things and love meeting people who are equally-motivated and bold to share their vision with the world.

I became fascinated by User Experience Design (UX) in 2016 when I started learning about processes, concepts and theories of UX/UI and product design through reading blogs, books and attending courses. Later I decided to take my education to the next level by completing MSc in Computing and IT with focus on Human-Computer Interactions. This degree helped me better understand the technical angle of the product lifecycle. Both consciously and subconsciously I started applying the UX principles to my projects, work and day to day life. I am a big-picture thinker and enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation all the way to development.

If you’d like to know more about me, my work, past projects or you’d just want to chat drop me an email at hello@barboradragunova.com.
