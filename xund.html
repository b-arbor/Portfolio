-- XUND --

Freemium Features for Mobile App

I ran a study for XUND, a healthcare startup, helping to determine workflow of the mobile app and the elements that are being included in the app’s freemium model as well as the available premium features.

My Role

In this project I was Product Designer, UX Researcher and Visual Designer. The aim of the project was determining the freemium features for XUND’s mobile app and the placement of the signup wall in the application’s workflow.

The Challenge

The biggest challenge of the project was fitting the study’s findings and proposed changes in the release timeline for the app in the App store (we made it).

The Study

I designed a quantitative questionnaire to validate the proposed concepts. This proved we are on the right track, and we were able to adjust the features according to the user’s needs.

This was followed by UI mockups incorporating these suggestions. It required quick turnaround time between me and the founders to ship the app on time.

Project Outcomes

We validated the features included in the freemium version of the app. The study helped us to make the final tweaks to the project.

We also reviewed the app flow and changed positioning of some features (such as signup flow) and mobile app is available in the App Store for selected regions.
