-- XUND --

Freemium Features for Mobile App

XUND is a Austrian start-up who created the symtom-checker I ran a study for XUND a healthcare startup, to determine workflow of the mobile app. My work helped the company decide which elements should be included in the app’s freemium model as well as the available premium features.

My Role

In this project I performed product and visual design, and conducted user research. The aim of the project was to determinine the freemium features for XUND’s mobile app and the placement of the signup wall in the application’s workflow.

The Challenge

The biggest challenge of the project was conducting the study and incorporating its findings into the app in accordance with the release timeline for the app in the App store. The recommended changes were added to the app on schedule, making the project a success.

The Study

I designed a quantitative questionnaire to validate the proposed concepts. The responses to the questionnaire proved the company was on the right track, and they were able to adjust the features to be more in line with the user’s needs.

The questionnaire was followed by UI mockups that incorporated these suggestions. In order to meet the limited timeline, the team had to communicate effectively and the study had to be conducted rapidly. Project Outcomes

We validated the features included in the free version of the app. The study helped the company make final tweaks to the project.

We also reviewed the app flow and changed positioning of some features, such as signup flow. The mobile app is now available in the App Store for Austria region.
